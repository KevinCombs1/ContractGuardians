Contract Law Education

Types of Contracts

Contracts may be written, oral, express, or implied. Understanding which type applies to your agreements is essential for evaluating enforceability. In Real Estate, common contracts include purchase agreements, leases, and service agreements. In Technology, software licensing, SaaS agreements, and developer contracts are critical.

Types of Law

Contracts are subject to various legal frameworks, including civil law, commercial law, and specific state statutes. Knowledge of the governing law is crucial to understand what provisions can be enforced or challenged.

Common Misrepresentations

Misrepresentation, incomplete disclosure, and unconscionable terms can impact the validity and enforceability of contracts. Our education modules explain these in detail so you can spot potential issues before entering binding agreements.
