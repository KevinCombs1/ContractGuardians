Contract Law Education

Types of Contracts

Contracts may be written, oral, express, or implied. Understanding each type is essential to evaluate enforceability. Real Estate contracts include purchase agreements, leases, and service contracts. Technology agreements involve SaaS licenses, software development contracts, and partnerships.

Types of Law

Contracts are influenced by civil law, commercial law, and state statutes. Knowing the governing law helps identify enforceable clauses and potential negotiation leverage.

Common Misrepresentations

Contracts may include misrepresentations, incomplete disclosure, or unconscionable terms. Our education modules teach you how to identify risks and negotiate better outcomes.
